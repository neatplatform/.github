# Code of Conduct

We aim to make contributing to NeatPlatform a positive experience for everyone.
Here are a few clear and encouraging principles:

  - **BE KIND.** Assume good intent and treat people the way you'd want to be treated.
  - **BE INCLUSIVE.** We welcome contributors of all backgrounds, identities, and experience levels.
  - **BE COLLABORATIVE.** Share what you know, ask when you don't, and help others along the way.
